About the Project

Norrath Re-imagined is a project focused on expanding and fleshing out the lore and history of the world of Norrath from the Massively Multiplayer Online Game, Everquest. The project will be delving into the various races of Norrath as well as expanding upon the stories and myths surrounding the Gods. At its core, Norrath Re-imagined is an Everquest fan site that will someday allow other fans to come together and discuss and experience the world of Norrath together.
